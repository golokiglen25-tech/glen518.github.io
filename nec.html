2026 NEC Reorganization

The 2026 National Electrical Code (NEC) has undergone its most significant reorganization in decades. For US-based electrical engineers and contractors, understanding these movements is critical for compliance and safety.

Key Movement: Article 120 & 130

The core of the reorganization involves moving high-frequency articles to Chapter 1 for easier access:

  • Article 120 (Load Calculations): Formerly Article 220, this has been moved to Chapter 1 to streamline the design process.
  • Article 130 (Energy Management): Formerly Article 750, this now sits in Chapter 1 to reflect the growing importance of smart grid technology.
Pro Tip: Re-tabbing your physical code book early will help you build authority with local inspectors and save time on-site.

Why It Matters

Mistakes in load calculations are the leading cause of rejected inspections. By mastering Article 120, you ensure your projects stay on schedule and under budget.
